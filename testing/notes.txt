--> Unit testing is used to verify logic, tipically at a function or method level.
    - TDD (Test-Driven Development) and BDD (Behavior-Driven Development)
    - Writing tests helps you think more critically about your application design choices 
        and helps you avoid pitfalls early on. 
        
--> Acceptance Testing: tipically used for web applications.
    - It envolves scripting control of a browser and attempting to trigger web application
        functionality.
